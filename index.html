The problem

Consumers do not need more products. They need better fashion decisions.

Nothing to wear

Users often own many clothes but struggle to see useful outfit combinations.

Impulse buying

Fashion platforms are designed to encourage more purchases, not better decisions.

Fragmented tools

Wardrobe apps know owned items. Shopping assistants know products. Closiq connects both.

Core product philosophy

From recommendation to evaluation.

Traditional platforms say: “You should buy this.” Closiq can say: “You do not need this.” That is the main innovation. The platform evaluates purchases through wardrobe compatibility, duplication risk, gap filling, cost-per-wear prediction, and style consistency.
