AI Fashion Decision Platform

The AI shopping agent that knows your wardrobe.

Closiq helps users make smarter fashion decisions based on what they already own. It combines a digital wardrobe, personal styling, and an AI shopping advisor in one calm mobile-first experience.

The problem

Most shopping apps are designed to make users buy more. Closiq helps users buy better.

01

Nothing to wear

Users own many clothes but struggle to build outfits and understand what their wardrobe is missing.

02

Decision fatigue

Fashion shopping requires balancing style, price, occasion, weather, and existing wardrobe compatibility.

03

Overconsumption

Traditional platforms recommend more products, even when the user may not actually need them.

Product experience

A mobile-first fashion assistant that feels calm, personal, and intelligent.

The core innovation

Buy or Don’t Buy.

The AI Verdict Engine is the heart of Closiq. Instead of only saying “you might like this,” the system evaluates whether a purchase truly improves the user’s wardrobe.

  • Duplication score
  • Wardrobe compatibility score
  • Gap-filling analysis
  • Cost-per-wear prediction
  • Style consistency check
